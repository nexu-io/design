FAQ

What you might want to know

What is Nexu? How does it relate to Refly?
Nexu is your digital clone hub, powered by Refly: preset roles, assign work, approve, track progress—all in one place. Refly provides the infrastructure; Nexu lets you assign and monitor clones from your collaboration tools.
Which tools are supported? How do I connect?
Slack and Feishu are supported today. In Integrations & settings, follow the flow to authorize Nexu Bot—takes a few minutes. After that, @ the bot in the channel to assign tasks or check status, no need to switch apps.
Can I change preset roles or add my own clones?
Yes. Preset roles (ops, dev, content, founder clone, etc.) are ready to use. You can also add custom clones with their own skills and knowledge bases for multi-clone collaboration.
What is “key-step approval”?
When a clone hits a step that needs your call (e.g. send externally, merge code, publish content), it shows up in the approval center. You approve or reject with feedback; the clone learns your preferences and aligns with your decisions over time.
Is the free tier enough? How does it differ from Pro?
Free is for trying it out: 50 requests/month, 1 IM channel, basic clone capabilities. Pro gives higher limits, unlimited channels, clone memory and preference learning, permanent deploy, and more—for daily use and teams.
Is my data and access secure?
Clones only access third-party tools within what you authorize (e.g. specific Linear projects, Slack channels). Permissions are configurable per clone; approvals and actions are auditable to meet common security and compliance needs.
